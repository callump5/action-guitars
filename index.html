About

Based in Leigh-on-Sea, Essex, I've spent 15 years repairing fretted instruments for local pickers, recording musicians and clients throughout the UK. I know from personal experience how nerve racking it is to find businesses that truly cares about the quality of service they provide. I can say that my continual effort to perform professional quality repairs has blessed me both personally and professionally. Services: Bass guitar setup - Bass guitar repair - Acoustic guitar repair - Guitar repair - Cracked headstock repair - Broken guitar head repair - Machine head replacement - Guitar knob replacement - Selector switch replacement - Pickup selector replacement - Guitar intonation - Intonation - Strap buttons - String tree fitting - Stringing service - Block replacement - Strat block replacement - Amp repair - Restringing Serving Areas: Southend - Rayeligh - Leigh on sea - Basildon - Canvey Island - Hockely - Essex
